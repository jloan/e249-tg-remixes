Remixing Taroko Gorge


In Spring 2019, a group of writers at Pierce College experimented with remixing Nick Monfort's "Taroko Gorge" as part of the first iteration of special projects course in Electronic Literature.

The remixes are collected here. When entering the collection, the first poem you will see is Monfort's original. To view the remixes follow the links along the right-hand side of the poem pages.
